Description

Raspberry Honey is one of the most fragrant honey varieties. The aroma is pleasant, like your favorite flower. Like other berry honeys it is light amber in color with a subtle but definite berry taste. It is delicate and smooth, perfect for use in teas and coffee. Raspberry honey also pairs well with chocolates desserts such as brownies and ice cream. It is delicious in salad dressings too.
