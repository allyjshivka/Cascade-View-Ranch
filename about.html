Our Story

The Farm

Our family loves the country life and sharing it with others. Join us for healthy food, creative crafts, enjoying nature and helping the community.

    Our Products

  • Food

    We rotate crops to bring seasonal fruits and vegetables to your table throughout the year while maximizing soil fertility.

  • Crafts

    We use inedible parts of the plants to create beautiful and useful gifts and household items.

  • Education

    Perhaps most of all, we enjoy sharing our knowledge and skills with others as we study nature, create beauty, bring children to the farm with our "Farm Kid for a Day" events, and bring the farm to hospitalized children with lovingly made gift baskets.

    Fun

  • Tours

    Farm tours are available upon request for school groups and others. To make arrangements, please call 518-543-2345.

  • Family

    The farm is a wonderful setting for family members of all ages to share healthy outdoor activities.

  • Fruit picking

    Pick your own seasonal fruits from strawberries to apples.

  • On-site shop

    In addition to our freshly-harvested food and our unique hand-made jewelry, we sell honey, maple syrup, candles and many other items.
